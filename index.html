Trail Riding Holiday

in the Hungarian countryside

Application form

Join our trail riding tours and experience freedom
in the South Hungarian countryside.

Three days on horseback, picnic lunch, wine dinner and traditional Hungarian hospitality
Taste the delicious wines of the region and enjoy the local gastronomy.

  • Day 1 Thursday

    You can meet one of our representatives in Pécs who will drive you to the Equestrian Center of Babarcszőlős. We can welcome our guests from 17 pm. After registration you are free to discover the surroundings, or to have a rest in a quiet guest room. Welcome dinner is served from 19 pm.

    next day

  • Day 2 Friday

    After breakfast we meet the horses and get brief instructions. We saddle the horses and start our tour to ride through the region between Szava and Hegyszentmárton. Lunch and dinner are provided by the equestrian center.

    previous day next day

  • Day 3 Saturday

    After breakfast we ride to the Drava River through a region of exceptional biodiversity. We have a picnic lunch near the river. We ride back to the equestrian center, and have a special wine dinner in the evening.

    previous day next day

  • Day 4 Sunday

    After breakfast we set off to the direction of Turony and stop for lunch at Tenkes Csárda, a traditional Hungarian tavern. Then we set off for our last riding. Transport from the center to Pécs is available for our guests in the late afternoon.

    previous day

ACCOMODATION

Our equestrian center is located in Babarcszőlős, in a small and quiet village near Pécs, where you can rest in tranquility. Our guests are accommodated in a newly built guesthouse in rooms with individual bathrooms.

OUR HORSES

We are awaiting our visitors with roughly thirty happy and healthy horses. We select a horse for each rider that will suit their skills and experience to enjoy trail riding with us at its fullest.

OUR TRAILS

Our trail rides take place in wide open meadows and in forests of diverse flora and wildlife. You can enjoy freedom on horseback and explore the beauties of nature during the lengthy routes.

    OUR EQUESTRIAN CENTER OFFERS

    • Guided rides in beautiful terrain
    • Especially long gallop routes
    • Well-trained horses
    • Riding equipment upon request
    • Accommodation in a tranquil environment
    • Three meals per day made of local farmers' goods
    • A special wine dinner with local wines and gastronomy
